# HDESK - Quick Employee Guide Video Script
**Duration: 3-4 minutes | Target Audience: All Employees**
---

## SCENE 1: INTRODUCTION
**[Duration: 15 seconds]**

**[Screen: Title card with HDESK logo]**

**Narrator:**
"Welcome to HDESK - your support portal for all IT and HR needs. Let me show you how to submit and track tickets in just a few simple steps."

---

## SCENE 2: LOGIN & DASHBOARD
**[Duration: 30 seconds]**

**[Screen: Shows browser opening and login page]**

**Narrator:**
"Access HDESK through your browser and log in with your employee username and password - the same credentials you use for your Harley credentials.

**[Screen: Dashboard loads]**

Once logged in, your dashboard shows all your tickets with color-coded priority levels: Red for HIGH priority with 30-minute response time, Yellow for MEDIUM with 4-hour response, and Green for LOW priority handled within one business day."

---

## SCENE 3: CREATING A TICKET
**[Duration: 1 minute]**

**[Screen: User clicks "Create New Ticket" button]**

**Narrator:**
"To create a ticket, click 'Create New Ticket'. 

**[Screen: Shows department selection]**

First, select your department - IT for technical issues or HR for payroll, leave, and employee matters.

**[Screen: Shows category selection]**

Next, choose your issue category. For IT: Hardware, Software, Network, Email, or System Access. For HR: Payroll, Leave, or Employee Concerns.

**[Screen: Shows ticket form]**

Now, provide a clear title and detailed description of your problem. The more specific you are, the faster we can help. Attach screenshots or files if needed - up to 5MB per file.

**[Screen: Submit button clicked, success message]**

Click Submit! You'll get a unique ticket number like TKT-2026-7330 and receive email notifications for all updates."

---

## SCENE 4: TRACKING & STATUS UPDATES
**[Duration: 45 seconds]**

**[Screen: Dashboard showing ticket list]**

**Narrator:**
"Track your tickets from the dashboard. Tickets move through five stages:

- **PENDING** (Yellow): Waiting for assignment
- **OPEN** (Blue): Assigned to a technician
- **IN PROGRESS** (Purple): Actively being worked on
- **RESOLVED** (Green): Fixed and awaiting your confirmation
- **CLOSED** (Gray): Confirmed and completed

**[Screen: Shows notification bell]**

You'll receive notifications in the app and via email for every update. Click any ticket to view details, add comments, or communicate with your assigned technician."

---

## SCENE 5: RESOLUTION & SLA
**[Duration: 30 seconds]**

**[Screen: Shows resolved ticket]**

**Narrator:**
"When your ticket is resolved, test the fix before confirming. If it works, click 'Confirm Resolution'. If the problem persists, click 'Reopen Ticket' with details about what's still wrong.

**[Screen: Shows SLA times]**

Our Service Level Agreement guarantees:
- HIGH priority: 30-minute response, 1-day resolution
- MEDIUM priority: 4-hour response, 2-3 days resolution  
- LOW priority: 1-day response, 3-5 days resolution

Business hours: Monday-Thursday 8 AM-4 PM, Friday 8 AM-4 PM. Tickets submitted after 4 PM Friday resume Monday at 8 AM."

---

## SCENE 6: QUICK TIPS & CLOSING
**[Duration: 30 seconds]**

**[Screen: Quick tips list]**

**Narrator:**
"Quick tips for best results:
- Use clear, specific titles
- Provide detailed descriptions
- Attach screenshots of errors
- Respond promptly to IT requests
- Set appropriate priority levels

**[Screen: HDESK logo and contact info]**

That's it! HDESK makes getting support simple and transparent. Access it anytime from any device. For urgent issues, create a HIGH priority ticket or call the IT hotline.

Ready to get started? Log in to HDESK today and experience fast, reliable support.

**[End Screen: Website URL and contact information]**"
